Exploring PH Arts

Learn various local arts

Films

This part shows how evident it is that the local films in the Philippines, and the Film festivals [which create jobs for many Filipino filmmakers] can significantly affect our economy. The Top 10 highest-grossing Filipino films and the common denominators that led to the success of these films are also discussed to further emphasize why local films must be promoted.

Visual Arts

This part will bring out the great reputation that the Philippines have in terms of creativity with the four Filipino visual artists, as well as the issues in this industry. Possible ways to promote the country's local visual artists including filling a bill, establishing art galleries, and professionalism are also shown to generate a great buzz around the market.

Culinary Arts

The essay will begin with a mini-survey (just for fun) to discover five interesting Filipino dishes, and why Culinary can be considered an art. The provided research, such as the positive experience of foreign tourists in the popular destination for culinary tourism, explains how ignoring our culinary arts can lead to lost opportunities that could have stimulated the Philippines' economic growth. Social media and Food vloggers are also highlighted in this part which can promote our Culinary Arts industry.

Festivals

This part covers the importance of festivals in Filipino culture. In this part, we will be using Actual Performances from Youtube as our format and we will also discuss their impact on the local economy and tourism. This will also explore the most popular festivals in the Philippines and discuss ways to promote them.
